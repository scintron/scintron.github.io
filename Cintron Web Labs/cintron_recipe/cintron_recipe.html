Three Ingredient Quesadilla

  • Two corn tortillas
  • One slice of cheddar or pepper jack cheese
  • Small bunch spinach

    • Optional:
  • Sour cream
  • Hot Sauce

Directions

  1. Place torilla on pre heated non stick pan.
  2. Put desired cheese on the tortilla. Make sure Cheese fits inside the margins of the tortilla.
  3. Place spinach on top.
  4. Once you see the cheese melt a bit, place the second torilla on top of the spinach.
  5. Flip
  6. Let cook for one more minute
  7. Plate and serve with sour cream and hot sauce if desired
